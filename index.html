I love Juggling!

It's good for the mind, body, and soul.

Juggling is many things and takes many forms, like poi and club passing. It goes by many names, such as spinning or circus arts. It is something you can do alone or with a group. Most importantly, it is exciting and challenging.

With a little bit of coaching and about 10 minutes of patience, anyone can learn to be a juggler too.

My name is Kerry (she/her), I am proud to be a juggler. I first took up this art form in high school and I continue to grow my skills today!
